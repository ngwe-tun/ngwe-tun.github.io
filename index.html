ABORTION IS A RIGHT!

The lack of safe and legal access to abortion can put women at risk physically and mentally.

Life-Threatening

Unsafe and Illegal abortion is a major cause of injury and death among women worldwide. It is estimated that nearly 25 million unsafe abortions take place annually.

Legalized or Decriminalized

As of June 2022, 64 countries have legalized or decriminalized abortion on request.

Imprisonment for Abortion

Women are sentenced up to 30 years in jail for having an abortion.

A Matter of Human Rights

Access to safe abortion services is a human right. Under international human rights law, everyone has a right to life, a right to health, and a right to be free from violence, discrimination, and torture or cruel, inhuman and degrading treatment.

How to Reduce
Illegal Abortion

The first strategy for preventing unsafe clandestine abortion is to reduce the incidence of unwanted pregnancy through measures such as improved access to effective contraception, post-abortion family planning counseling, health education campaigns aimed at promoting condom use among young people.
